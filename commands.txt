Pthreads:

    Build:
        gcc pthread/pth-imp.c -o pthread/pth-imp

    Run:
        sbatch full-pth.sh

MPI:

    Build:
        mpicc -o mpi MPI_C_SAMPLE.c

    Run:
        sbatch full-mpi.sh

Open MPI:

    Build:
        mpicc /opmpi/opmpi_imp.c -o opmpi_imp.exe

    Run:
        sbatch full-opmpi.sh
