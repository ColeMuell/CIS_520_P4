Pthreads:

    Build:
        gcc pthread/pth-imp.c -o pthread/pth-imp

    Run:
        sbatch full-pth.sh

MPI:

    Build:
        mpicc ./mpi/mpi-imp.c -o mpi/mpi-imp

    Run:
        sbatch full-mpi.sh

Open MPI:

    Build:
        mpicc ./opmpi/opmpi-imp.c -lgomp -o opmpi/opmpi-imp

    Run:
        sbatch full-opmpi.sh
